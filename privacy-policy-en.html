Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 14, 2025

Welcome to FaceGlow (hereinafter referred to as "the App" or "we"). We highly value your privacy protection and personal information security. This Privacy Policy will help you understand how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information, especially regarding the processing of facial data.

Important Notice:

Before using this App, please carefully read and fully understand all contents of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please stop using the App immediately.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Facial Data Collection

The App collects photos containing facial information that you upload for AI face-swapping and beautification processing. This specifically includes:

  • Facial Images: Selfie photos and portrait photos that you actively upload
  • Facial Feature Data: Facial key points, contours, and other feature information extracted from photos
  • Processing Results: Beautified or face-swapped photos generated through AI processing

1.2 Other Information Collection

  • Account Information: Phone number (for registration and login), User ID
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App usage duration, feature usage patterns, operation logs
  • Subscription Information: Subscription type, purchase records, expiration dates
  • Network Information: IP address, network type (WiFi/mobile network)

2. Purpose of Using Facial Data

2.1 Core Service Functions

  • AI Face-Swapping: Using your uploaded facial photos for AI model training and face-swapping processing to generate artistic photos
  • Beautification Effects: Applying beautification and enhancement processing to photos to improve photo quality
  • Template Matching: Matching your facial features with preset templates to generate photos in various styles
  • Effect Preview: Showing you before-and-after comparison effects

2.2 Service Optimization

  • Algorithm Optimization: Using de-identified facial data to improve AI algorithms and enhance processing effectiveness and accuracy
  • Quality Enhancement: Analyzing failed processing cases to optimize model performance
  • Feature Development: Developing new AI features and template styles

2.3 User Experience

  • History Records: Saving your work history for convenient viewing and downloading anytime
  • Personalized Recommendations: Recommending suitable templates based on your usage preferences
  • Technical Support: Providing technical assistance when you encounter issues

3. Facial Data Sharing and Third Parties

3.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your facial data with the following third-party service providers, but only to the extent necessary to provide services:

  • Tencent Cloud COS (Object Storage):
    • Purpose: Storing your uploaded photos and generated works
    • Data Transfer: Using HTTPS encrypted transmission
    • Storage Location: Mainland China servers
    • Security Measures: Enterprise-level security protection provided by Tencent Cloud
  • AI Processing Service:
    • Purpose: Executing AI face-swapping and beautification algorithms
    • Processing Method: Processing in a secure cloud environment
    • Data Usage: Only used for current processing, not for other purposes

3.2 What We Will Never Do

  • No Selling: We will never sell your facial data to any third party
  • No Public Display: We will not publicly display your photos without your explicit consent
  • No Misuse: We will not use your facial data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • No Transfer: We will not transfer facial data to any other companies or organizations

3.3 Legal Disclosure Requirements

We may need to disclose your information in the following circumstances:

  • As required by laws and regulations
  • Upon request from judicial authorities or government departments
  • When necessary to protect our or users' legitimate rights and interests
  • In response to emergency situations (such as threats to personal safety)

4. Facial Data Storage Location and Security

4.1 Storage Locations

  • Server Location: Mainland China (Tencent Cloud servers)
  • Local Storage: Downloaded works are saved in your device's local album
  • Temporary Storage: Temporary data during processing is stored in the cloud

4.2 Security Measures

We implement the following security measures to protect your facial data:

  • Encrypted Transmission: Using HTTPS/TLS protocols to encrypt all data transmissions
  • Encrypted Storage: Encrypting stored photos and data
  • Access Control: Strictly limiting employee access permissions, with access only for authorized personnel
  • Security Audits: Conducting regular security assessments and vulnerability scans
  • Backup Mechanism: Regular data backups to prevent data loss
  • Protection Measures: Deploying firewalls, intrusion detection, and other security protection systems

5. Facial Data Retention Period

5.1 Retention Duration

  • Uploaded Original Photos:
    • Selfie photos: Retained until you actively delete them
    • Temporary processing photos: Deleted immediately after processing completion
  • Generated Works:
    • Retained until you actively delete them
    • Retained for 30 days after account cancellation, then permanently deleted
  • Facial Feature Data:
    • Deleted within 24 hours after processing completion
    • Only used for current processing, not stored long-term
  • Data for Algorithm Optimization:
    • Retained after complete de-identification processing
    • Cannot identify individuals
    • Retention period not exceeding 2 years

5.2 Deletion Mechanism

You can delete your data at any time through the following methods:

  • Delete individual works or selfie photos within the app
  • Clear all works in the personal center
  • Cancel your account (which will delete all related data)
  • Contact customer service to request deletion (privacy@faceglow.app)

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your facial data and personal information:

6.1 Right of Access

  • View all your data that we collect and store
  • Understand the purpose and method of data usage

6.2 Right of Rectification

  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Update your account information

6.3 Right of Deletion

  • Delete your uploaded photos and generated works
  • Request deletion of all personal data
  • Cancel your account and delete all information

6.4 Right to Withdraw Consent

  • Withdraw your consent to data processing at any time
  • Stop using specific features

6.5 Right to Data Portability

  • Download all your works to local devices
  • Export a copy of your personal data

6.6 Right to Complain

  • File complaints with us about data processing issues
  • File complaints with regulatory authorities

7. Protection of Minors

We take the privacy protection of minors very seriously:

  • This App is intended for users aged 18 and above
  • If you are a minor, please use it with the consent and guidance of your guardian
  • We do not knowingly collect facial data from minors
  • If we discover that we have collected data from minors, we will delete it immediately
  • Guardians have the right to request deletion of all data belonging to minors

8. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use the following technologies to improve user experience:

  • Local Storage: Saving your preference settings and login status
  • Analytics Tools: Understanding app usage (anonymous data)
  • Crash Reports: Collecting crash logs to improve app stability

These technologies are not used to collect facial data, only for app functionality and performance optimization.

9. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes to the policy, we will:

  • Notify you through pop-ups or notifications within the app
  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Seek your explicit consent for significant changes

We recommend that you review this policy regularly to stay informed of the latest information.

10. International Data Transfer

Currently, all our data processing and storage takes place within Mainland China. If cross-border data transfer is needed in the future, we will:

  • Obtain your explicit consent in advance
  • Ensure that the destination country/region has an adequate level of data protection
  • Take appropriate security measures to protect data
  • Comply with relevant laws and regulations
